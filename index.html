Instructions

  1. Once you hit the play game button you will be taken to the game page.
  2. On this page you will be given a countries flag with four different conutries options to choose from.
  3. Pick the correct flag to the country that owns it.
  4. Once you have made your pick and click on the countries name and game will show you the correct answer. If you are right you get 10 points and you are wrong you lose 5 points.
  5. The aim of the game is to get all the flags correct.
  6. Also the is a countdown timer so you don't have long to make a pick. Once the time running out you move on to the next question.
  7. At the end of the game you will find out your score.
  8. Good Luck!!!
